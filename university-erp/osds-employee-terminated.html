Employee Termination Notice

An employee has been terminated. This individual is terminated or removed from his/her position on working at the OSDS. The account, however, can still be accessed by the admin user for data retrieval and other purposes.

Note: There will be a 7-day allowance before the account of the employee is going to be permanently removed to the registry of OSDS.

By checking the box, you firmly ensure and understand the termination of an employee.

Settings: Access the Terminated Employee Tab

You may go back 'Home' or 'Administrator'.
